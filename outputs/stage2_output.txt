STAGE 2: LLM + RAG / Tools
======================================================================

[How it works]
  1. LLM receives tools (search_legal_database, calculate_damages)
  2. LLM decides which tools to call and with what arguments
  3. We execute the tools and feed results back to the LLM
  4. LLM generates a final answer grounded in retrieved data

The statute of limitations for a contract breach is **4 years**, as per **UCC § 2-725**. This means that a party must file a lawsuit within 4 years from the date the breach occurred or was discovered. If you have further questions or need assistance with a specific case, feel free to ask!
